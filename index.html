Fruits for health

Apples - High in fiber, antioxidants, and vitamin C, they help support heart health and digestion. Bananas - A good source of potassium, vitamin B6, and fiber, bananas help maintain heart health and aid in digestion. Oranges - Packed with vitamin C, oranges boost the immune system, improve skin health, and enhance iron absorption. Blueberries - Rich in antioxidants, they help protect against oxidative stress, support brain health, and improve heart health. Avocados - High in healthy fats, avocados support heart health, skin health, and reduce inflammation. Strawberries - Full of vitamin C and antioxidants, strawberries promote skin health, support immune function, and reduce inflammation. Grapes - Rich in antioxidants like resveratrol, grapes support heart health, reduce inflammation, and improve cognitive function.
